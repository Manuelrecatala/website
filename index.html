Manuel Recatalà

A fun and cooperative atmosphere on set, where everyone feels heard and appreciated, is what I value most.

I am an editor and young director who loves the pressure of a film production and aims to achieve the best possible result within its tight timeframes.
My background as an editor leads me to focus on seamless cuts and unpredictable solutions that capture the viewer’s attention. As a director, dynamic storytelling is what truly drives me. Designing a narrative through my own voice and watching it come together, is where I find real joy and satisfaction.
I’ve been working with AI and continue to study it as a creative tool, exploring how it can support my craft without losing its human touch.
